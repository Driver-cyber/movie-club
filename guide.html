05

The shared verdict

The Shared tab blends your scores into one verdict, shows each of your averages, and reveals both sets of notes. Sort by most recent or top rated.

It even flags your biggest split — the film you two disagreed on most.
Share with your other phone
Turn on sync here, then open the invite link on your partner's phone. No account, no setup.
Turn on cross-phone sync
06

Link your two phones

On the Shared tab, tap Turn on cross-phone sync. The app quietly sets up a shared list for the two of you — no accounts, no logins, nothing to install.

07

Send the invite

Tap Copy invite link and text it to each other. Open it on the second phone — that's it. Both phones now share one list, updating whenever you reopen the app.

08

Never lose a rating

Every phone keeps its own copy, and Export data saves a backup file you can stash anywhere. Import brings it back — even into future versions of the app.

A quick Export now and then is a nice safety net.
